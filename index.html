About Us

Students for the Exploration and Development of Space (SEDS) is a non-profit that empowers young people to participate and make an impact in space exploration

SEDS COSMOS is an independent, student-run, non-profit organization driven by the common goal to not only educate people about space and its benefits but also help impart leadership and management skills to its members via indulging in events and projects closely related to space sciences and technology.
