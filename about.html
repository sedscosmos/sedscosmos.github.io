The core committee is responsible for conducting various space-themed conferences, events, and competitions within our campus for students from colleges all over the country. It also collaborates with SEDS Nepal for national-level events which allow the participants to see the space industry as an exciting and viable career option.

Our Outreach programme extends to the local schools in and around Lalitpur where we teach the students about how the universe works using interactive teaching methods and an in-depth curriculum. The programme helps us stay true to our mission of arousing interest in space exploration among young students from all backgrounds and empowering them to pursue it further.
